    I am Software Engineer with a strong interest in building practical and user-friendly digital solutions. During my school years, and since then, I have explored various aspects of software development including web systems, automation tools, and Game Development. With hands-on experience in both solo based projects, I believe technology can be a powerful tool to solve real-world problems and improve everyday life.

    My Journey?

    Start on interest in game development began during primary school, where I created my first simple game using Scratch—a basic car game controlled by the keyboard. This early experience sparked my passion for building interactive projects. Around 2018–2019, I started learning Unity after being inspired by Dani’s game Karlson. I deepened my knowledge through online tutorials, particularly from Brackeys, and gradually developed skills in both 2D and 3D game development. Over the years, I have created multiple playable prototypes, focusing on improving my programming, game mechanics, and problem-solving skills. Although these projects were not fully released, they reflect my continuous learning and hands-on experience. Beyond game development, I have also explored other areas of programming, including web development, Android development, and general software development. This has helped me gain a broader understanding of the IT field and strengthened my overall technical skills. After taking a break caused by a broken laptop and hardware limitations, I later resumed my development journey with renewed motivation. More recently, I have expanded my skill set by exploring Roblox Studio, where I am currently working on new game projects.
